508 Malka Heifetz Tussman

With a Fool

Culture—

What does a fool do with culture?

What does a cultured fool do?

With culture, he can wreck the world.
And strange—I can’t be clever with a fool.
I become more foolish than he.

I'm afraid of a fool,

and I have no idea

how this fool fastened on

to me.

Marcia Falk
